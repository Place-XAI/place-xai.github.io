Core Conclusion

The holographic interpretation of wormhole dynamics requires asking only about specific processes — not about the Hamiltonian as a whole. The two-sided teleportation process in the original experiment does have a traversable wormhole interpretation via the Sachdev–Ye–Kitaev (SYK) model, even though many other processes applied to the same learned Hamiltonian do not. The key insight is that the SYK Hamiltonian itself is not "gravitational" in all regimes — only certain low-temperature correlation functions are dominated by the Schwarzian mode, and a dual gravitational description applies only to specific, well-defined processes.
